I have spent my career moving from physical hardware to the cloud, always obsessed with one thing: Reliability. Today, I bridge the gap between traditional DevOps and the future of AI. By integrating Agentic AI (Claude, LangChain) into the DevOps lifecycle, I help companies move away from "firefighting" and toward a state of automated, self-healing infrastructure.

My approach to infrastructure focuses on three core pillars:

  • Efficiency: Using Terraform to slash configuration time by ~60%.
  • Stability: Building CI/CD pipelines that eliminate manual, error-prone deployments.
  • Intelligence: Leveraging Agentic AI to intelligently optimize AWS costs and workflows.

Beyond engineering, I co-mentor the next generation of professionals at DevOps Micro Internship (DMI), guiding engineers across Africa through production-grade microservices deployments.

If your team needs an engineer who can architect, automate, and document production infrastructure that scales without the stress — I am open to Remote roles.
